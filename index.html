Aavishkar’24

Aavishkar, launched by KURC in 2017, is an annual robotics festival promoting Nepali youth in robotics through workshops, exhibitions, and competitions. Organized by KURC, it aims to foster innovation and attract global participants for educational and entertaining experiences.

Our Goals

Aavishkar’s goal is to ignite innovation and passion in robotics. Organized by KURC, it fosters Nepali youth’s talents, promoting workshops, exhibitions, and competitions. Aiming for global reach, it advocates for education, entertainment, and progress in robotics.

Pioneering Transformative Technology

Our vision is to harness transformative technology, empowering individuals and organizations to address global challenges and foster a connected, inclusive world. We strive for technological advancements that positively impact lives and drive a sustainable, prosperous future for all.

Igniting Innovation in Robotics

Aavishkar, initiated in 2017 by Kathmandu University Robotics Club (KURC), is an annual robotics festival igniting creativity and talent. The event motivates Nepal’s youth to excel in robotics, offering a global platform for innovation and education through various engaging activities and competitions.

Empowering the Robotics Enthusiasts

Aavishkar aims to empower aspiring creators, students, and technology enthusiasts by showcasing their projects and fostering a passion for robotics. This festival, featuring diverse themes and competitions, is a dynamic hub that connects participants worldwide, promoting innovation and education in the exciting realm of robotics.
